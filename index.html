Yogesh Chawla

Graduate Research Assistant · Robotics · Autonomous Systems

I am pursuing a master’s degree at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln in the Biological Systems Engineering department, where I work as a Graduate Research Assistant (GRA) in the MAARS Lab.

I completed a dual-degree program at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, majoring in Agricultural and Food Engineering with a micro-specialisation in Artificial Intelligence. My research vision has been shaped by my experience working at the University of Calgary, Iowa State University, and Purdue University.

My research interests lie in robotics — teaching systems to see, think, and move autonomously.
